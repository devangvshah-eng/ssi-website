Milestones

SSI Achieves Milestones in Maritime Safety

Unveiling Asia's First Sea Survival Centre to impart World-Class Training at ATI-ONGC, Goa

Water Survival Training Facility — INS Garuda, Kochi (2013)

SSI commissioned a Water Survival Training Facility (WSTF) for the Indian Navy at INS Garuda, Kochi on a turnkey basis in August 2013. The WSTF is a state-of-the-art facility, first in Asia. Fitted with 12 simulators which work in coordination to allow for environmental simulation — day and night conditions, wind, rain, waves, sound, thunder, lightning, wave generator — while conducting rigorous survival exercises.

Sea Survival Centre — ATI-ONGC, Goa (2024)

SSI commissioned the Sea Survival Centre at ATI-ONGC, Goa in January 2024. A world-class training facility, it is fitted with 10 simulators which work in coordination to allow for a 16-seater HUET ditching with environmental simulation including day and night conditions, wind, rain, waves, thunder and lightning while conducting rigorous survival exercises.
