About the Kochi Facility

The Water Survival Training Facility (WSTF), inaugurated at INS Garuda, Kochi in August 2013.

SSI commissioned this state-of-the-art facility — first of its kind in Asia — for the Indian Navy on a turnkey basis. The facility is fitted with 12 advanced simulators which work in coordination to allow for environmental simulation including day and night conditions, wind, rain, waves, sound, thunder, and lightning.

Key equipment includes: Helicopter Underwater Egress Trainer (HUET), Cockpit Underwater Egress Trainer (CUET), Parachute Drag and Disengagement Trainers (PDDT), and Rescue Hoist Trainer — providing rigorous multi-environment survival training.
