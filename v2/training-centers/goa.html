About the Goa Facility

ONGC backed sea survival training centre, inaugurated by PM Modi on February 5th, 2024.

Built by Mumbai-based Survival Systems India, this one of a kind and highly advanced training facility has one of the most advanced Survival Training Simulation Theatres in the world. The facility is now open for bookings.

The Integrated Survival Training Facility (ISTF) comprises the following simulators: Helicopter Underwater Egress Trainer (HUET), Rescue Hoist Trainer, Wave Simulator, and Environment Simulators.

The HUET is a modular egress training simulator designed to train aircrew and passengers in escaping from a sinking helicopter. The simulator can be quickly reconfigured by use of exterior panels which replicate the actual escape exits found on specific helicopters such as AW139, Bell 412, and Dauphin N3.

The Rescue Hoist Trainer (RHT) is designed to train passengers in rescue procedures and techniques while being rescued by a helicopter.
