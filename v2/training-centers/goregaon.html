About the Facility

Established in 2004. Mumbai's premier HUET training centre.

We deliver on-site training at our training centre located at Malad (West), Mumbai — 20–25 minutes away from the International/Domestic Airport. In the heart of the city with easy access from the airport, railway station, Metro, and more.

The centre features ample space with dedicated areas earmarked for each theory and practical training session. Our 16-seater HUET Simulator simulates real-life ditching scenarios with authentic controls and consoles.

Three Dedicated Classrooms Include:

  • Conference room style seating
  • Computers and projection facilities
  • Real life jackets with in-class inflation
  • Real Emergency Breathing System (EBS) donning
  • Passenger seats and harnesses
  • Marine & aviation life raft survival kit displays
