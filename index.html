Who We Are

SSI: Excellence in safety training since 2004.

Survival Systems India (SSI) is a key survival training provider in India, established in March 2004 following a 2003 helicopter crash at Bombay High. We operate three 16-seater HUET Simulators across India.

With a client base of 386+ companies across Oil & Gas, Marine, Aviation, and Naval sectors, SSI has recently been recognised as "Industry Champions" by OPITO among 230+ approved training centres globally.
