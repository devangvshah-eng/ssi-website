WHO WE ARE

India's Premier Sea Survival Training Institution

Survival Systems India (SSI) is the country's leading HUET (Helicopter Underwater Escape Training) operator, operating three 16-seater HUET Simulators across multiple locations in India.

We go beyond regulatory requirements — our tailored, scenario-based practical training programs are designed specifically for energy sector workers, offshore personnel, and aviation crews. With India's most advanced facilities and an uncompromising safety culture, SSI sets the gold standard for sea survival training.
