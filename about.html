Who We Are

Proudly serving India with state-of-the-art facilities.

Survival Systems India (SSI) is a key survival training provider in India, established in March 2004 following a 2003 helicopter crash at Bombay High. We are India's most trusted HUET operator, recognised globally by OPITO as "Industry Champions."

Our client base spans 386+ companies across Oil & Gas, Marine, Aviation, and Naval sectors — with three 16-seater HUET simulators operating across Goa, Mumbai, and Kochi.

Why Choose SSI

SSI: Excellence in safety training since 2004.

From regulatory compliance to unmatched infrastructure, SSI sets the gold standard for sea survival training in India.

01

OPITO Industry Champions

Recognised as "Industry Champions" by OPITO among 230+ approved training centres globally — the highest distinction available.

02

1:1 Instructor-Delegate Ratio

Every underwater exercise maintains a strict 1:1 ratio with HUET Divers, Seatbelt Safety Release, and Airgap protocols for maximum safety.

03

5-Tier MERP System

A comprehensive 5-Tier Medical Emergency Response Planning system is in place at all facilities, ensuring rapid response to any situation.

04

Three 16-Seater Simulators

India's only operator with three full-scale 16-seater HUET Simulators — across Goa, Goregaon (Mumbai), and Kochi.
